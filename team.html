Tony Cox: Lawyer

"I believe a happy office is a productive office. It has always been my goal to provide a friendly and professional service and, with the excellent support of my team, we are definitely achieving this."

Tony is well known for being down-to-earth, friendly and welcoming. He has a broad and loyal clientele who have no hesitation in recommending Tony for any legal needs.

Tony has experience in a comprehensive range of legal areas, which has not only provided him with a wealth of knowledge in his areas of interest but has afforded him a standing as a well-respected lawyer. He has been practising law in Port Macquarie since 1995 and over the years has gained a reputation as being amicable and fair, yet discerning, whether advocate or adversary.

You can get in touch with Tony at tony@coxlaw.com.au.

Nick Gainsford: Lawyer

"I became a lawyer because I wanted to use my knowledge of the legal system to assist others in understanding the avenues available to them."

After 20 years working for various government departments (10 years locally), Nick decided to put his legal expertise to good use. He assists Tony with all areas of law, with a focus on unfair dismissal, personal injury, worker's compensation, and court representation.

You can get in touch with Nick at nick@coxlaw.com.au.

Gina McNeil: Licensed Conveyancer

"Having strong ties to this community, I love meeting new people and assisting them in starting a new chapter in their lives."

Gina has worked locally in the conveyancing industry for fifteen years. After completing her studies at university, she became a Licensed Conveyancer in 2013. Gina has a wealth of experience in all aspects of conveyancing, particularly major developments and subdivisions.

You can get in touch with Gina at gina@coxlaw.com.au.

Ann Blythe: Licensed Conveyancer

"I love working with this team because we work well together and are all on the same page - committed to providing excellent service and advice to our clients."

Ann has lived in Port Macquarie for over 40 years. She started her career in the legal industry over 25 years ago, working as a legal secretary for a local law firm. This position fostered her passion for conveyancing and, in 2009 after graduating from Macquarie University with a Diploma in Conveyancing Law & Practice, Ann obtained her unrestricted Conveyancer’s Licence.

Since becoming a Licensed Conveyancer, Ann’s personal philosophy has been to provide her clients with a stress-free experience by offering a friendly, yet professional, service together with expert support, advice and assistance.

Ann is well-known for her sunny disposition and is popular amongst her peers. Being a local girl, she also has the knowledge of the area to compliment her conveyancing expertise. Ann is also available to assist you with any JP needs, having been a Justice of the Peace since 1989.

You can get in touch with Ann at ann@coxlaw.com.au.
